üé® RGB vs HEX Color Formats in ImGui

RGB and HEX are color formats. ImGui uses float-based RGB values like ImVec4. You must convert HEX or RGB into float values.

üìå Example Conversion

#55D7C6 ‚Üí RGB(85, 215, 198)
ImGui Float: ImVec4(85/255.0f, 215/255.0f, 198/255.0f, 1.0f)
            = ImVec4(0.33f, 0.84f, 0.78f, 1.0f)

‚úÖ Use in ImGui

ImVec4 myColor = ImVec4(0.33f, 0.84f, 0.78f, 1.0f);
ImGui::ColorEdit3("Pick Color", (float*)&myColor);
